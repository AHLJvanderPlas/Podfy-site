Responsible Disclosure

If you believe you’ve found a security vulnerability, please email [security@yourdomain] with details. Do not publicly disclose until we’ve had a chance to investigate and fix. We aim to acknowledge within 3 business days.

  • Make a good-faith effort to avoid privacy violations and service disruption.
  • Don’t access more data than necessary to demonstrate an issue.
  • We won’t pursue legal action for good-faith research aligned with this policy.
