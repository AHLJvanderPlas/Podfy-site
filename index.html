How it works

1. Assign

Create a customer slug (e.g., acme). Share the link or QR with your driver.

2. Capture

Driver snaps the signed note or uploads a PDF. Auto-compression and cleanup included.

3. Retrieve

Brand owner sees the POD instantly with logo/color applied and can search, preview, and export.

Built for brand owners
