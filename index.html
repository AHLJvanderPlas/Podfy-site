How it works

1) Assign

Brand owner creates a link (slug) for a customer and assigns it to the driver.

2) Capture

Driver uploads the signed POD via the link, no login required. Uploads use short-lived signed URLs.

3) Retrieve

Brand owner retrieves the POD instantly with customer branding (logo & color) applied.
